Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

What this tool does

HTML to PDF converts HTML files to PDF documents entirely in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server — all processing happens locally on your device.

Third-party services

This site does not use Google AdSense or other advertising services. The HTML-to-PDF conversion flow runs entirely in your browser without uploading files to a server.
